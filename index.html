About HTA

The Honeytreat Trade Academy is a private vocational training institute in construction trade, aimed at helping the youth, existing construction trade workers and artisans acquire perfect skills, competence, and certification in their various trades for better economic benefit.

We have set out to provide the needed internationally acceptable training in the vocational sector in Nigeria. We have been achieving this using a blended learning program which is a combination of classroom-styled teaching, hands-on workshop sessions and extensive internship with reputable organizations.
